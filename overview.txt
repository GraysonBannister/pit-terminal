Prediction Intelligence Terminal — Framework

Core Thesis

Most prediction markets are not efficiently pricing reality.

They are pricing:

* attention
* narrative momentum
* emotional conviction
* fragmented information
* delayed propagation

The system’s job is not:
“predict the future.”

The system’s job is:
“detect when market-implied probability materially diverges from reality-adjusted probability.”

This is fundamentally an information infrastructure business.
